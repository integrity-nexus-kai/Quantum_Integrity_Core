---

# 1. Purpose

This document defines the structural organization strategy for the TIG theory layer.

The objective is not expansion of theoretical scope.

The objective is:

- hierarchical separation,
- primitive isolation,
- dependency control,
- and prevention of structural layer contamination.

The framework now explicitly prioritizes:

> architectural clarity before theoretical expansion.

---

# 2. Structural Motivation

As the TIG framework evolved, the theory layer accumulated:

- primitive definitions,
- recursive structure analysis,
- admissibility evolution,
- candidate dynamics,
- and interpretational clarification.

Without explicit structural organization,
the framework risks:

- semantic mixing,
- hidden dependency loops,
- primitive contamination,
- and unstable conceptual hierarchy.

The present structure guide therefore defines:

> explicit separation between theoretical layers.

---

# 3. Core Structural Principle

Different theoretical layers must remain structurally separated.

In particular:

- primitives must not depend on dynamics,
- interpretational layers must not redefine primitives,
- and exploratory structures must not contaminate formal foundations.
