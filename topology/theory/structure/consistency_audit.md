# 1. Purpose

This document performs a preliminary structural consistency audit of the TIG framework (Topological Integrity Gravity).

The purpose is not expansion of the framework.

The purpose is:

- identification of structural inconsistencies,
- reduction of semantic ambiguity,
- detection of hidden assumptions,
- and clarification of primitive dependencies.

The framework now explicitly prioritizes:

> consistency before expansion.

---

# 2. Structural Motivation

As theoretical structure increases, the probability of hidden inconsistency also increases.

Potential failure modes include:

- circular definitions,
- primitive inflation,
- semantic duplication,
- hidden assumptions,
- undefined operators,
- and structurally uncontrolled abstraction.

The present audit therefore investigates:

> whether the current framework remains internally coherent under reduction-oriented analysis.
