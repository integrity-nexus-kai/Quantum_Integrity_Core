---

# 1. Purpose

This document defines the current non-claims, interpretational limits, and scope restrictions of the TIG framework (Topological Integrity Gravity).

The purpose is not reduction of ambition.

The purpose is:

- preservation of mathematical discipline,
- prevention of interpretational overreach,
- reduction of semantic inflation,
- and clarification of current theory status.

The framework now explicitly prioritizes:

> structural consistency over unrestricted explanatory scope.

---

# 2. Structural Motivation

As exploratory frameworks evolve, there is a persistent risk that:

- heuristic interpretation,
- structural analogy,
- or provisional mathematical structure

may be unintentionally misrepresented as finalized physical theory.

This document therefore defines:

> what TIG currently does NOT claim.
