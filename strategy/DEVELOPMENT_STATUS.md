# TIG Development Status

Topological Integrity Gravity (TIG) represents the physical admissibility layer of the Integrity Nexus research architecture.

The current public TIG repository contains the foundational formulation of TIG as a structurally constrained extension of General Relativity focused on horizon formation, regularized curvature behavior, and critical transition structures.

Since the initial publication state, the framework has undergone substantial conceptual and mathematical development.

These later developments are not treated as an uncontrolled replacement of TIG, but as a higher-level consolidation process.

## Current Governance Position

- TIG remains the canonical physical admissibility layer.
- SIR provides the mathematical abstraction and recursive admissibility layer.
- SGI is reserved for technical enforcement and implementation structures.

## Relation to SIR

Advanced recursive admissibility concepts, primitive stabilization, recursive operator structures, and cross-layer semantic governance are now consolidated in:

Structural Integrity Recursion (SIR)  
https://github.com/integrity-nexus-kai/Structural_Integrity_Recursion

SIR does not replace TIG.

SIR abstracts and governs the recursive and mathematical structures that emerged from TIG and related integrity frameworks.

## Publication Status

The published TIG material should be treated as the foundational TIG reference layer.

Further developments should be classified before integration:

- canonical
- exploratory
- speculative
- deprecated

Only mathematically stabilized material should migrate back into TIG as canonical physical content.
