I’ve been working on a structurally constrained extension of General Relativity called Topological Integrity Gravity (TIG).

The core idea is that horizon formation is not continuously deformable but governed by a critical transition, leading to non-linear observable behavior.

There’s a simple bifurcation structure behind it, which produces a clear difference from GR near criticality.
