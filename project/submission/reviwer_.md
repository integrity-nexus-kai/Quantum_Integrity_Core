# Reviewer Anticipation – TIG Paper

## 1. Metric Ansatz

Criticism:
"The metric is not derived from the field equations."

Response:
We explicitly state that the metric is an effective ansatz motivated by the scalar curvature profile in quadratic f(R) gravity.

Future work:
Full numerical solutions of the modified field equations.

---

## 2. QNM Calculation

Criticism:
"QNM results are approximate."

Response:
We use standard eikonal approximation linked to photon-sphere dynamics (Berti et al. 2009).

Positioning:
Qualitative trends, not precision spectroscopy.

---

## 3. Echo Physics

Criticism:
"Reflective boundary condition is ad hoc."

Response:
We adopt the standard phenomenological approach used in ECO models (Cardoso et al.).

---

## 4. Stability

Criticism:
"Incomplete stability analysis."

Response:
We confirm scalar stability (μ > 0) and clearly state that full dynamical analysis is future work.

---

## 5. Novelty

Core Claim:
Existence of a critical parameter βc governing horizon formation.

Key Positioning:
This represents a structural transition rather than a continuous deformation.

---

## 6. Observational Relevance

Position:
Order-of-magnitude consistency with GW150914.

No overclaim:
We do NOT claim detection, only potential deviations.
