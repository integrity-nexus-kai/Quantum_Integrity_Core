\section{Black Hole Solution in TIG}

We consider a static, spherically symmetric spacetime:

ds^2 = -f(r) dt^2 + f(r)^{-1} dr^2 + r^2 d\Omega^2

The TIG-modified field equations are:

G_{\mu\nu} + \alpha I_{\mu\nu} = 8\pi T_{\mu\nu}

We obtain a modified metric function:

f(r) = 1 - \frac{2GM}{r} + \epsilon(r)

where \epsilon(r) encodes the integrity correction.
