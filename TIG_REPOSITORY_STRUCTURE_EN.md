## 🏷️ Naming Convention

### Files

- use snake_case  
- avoid spaces  
- descriptive naming  

**Examples:**
- `black_hole_solution.md`
- `field_equations.md`
- `spectral_action.tex`

---

### Versioning

- `v1.0`, `v1.1` for stable versions  
- `draft_` prefix for work-in-progress
